PP Life Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Introduction

PP Life ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by PPLife. We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Data Collection and Storage

PP Life does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. All data, including your health and wellness data, is stored locally on your device only.

Health Data

PP Life accesses your health data through Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. This data is used solely to display health insights within the app and is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

Subscriptions

PP Life offers auto-renewable subscriptions to unlock premium features. Subscription payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

PP Life does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

PP Life is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
